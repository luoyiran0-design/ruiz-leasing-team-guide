Before Move-In

Utility Connection Guide

Use this page to arrange electricity, gas, internet, and any building-specific utility connection before you move in. Utility accounts must be connected under the tenant's name unless your lease or building arrangement says otherwise.

Start Utility Setup Find Property Manager

Tenant Responsibility

Utilities Are Connected by the Tenant

01 Lease is signed

Your tenancy is ready for move-in preparation.

02 Tenant chooses provider

Utilities must be connected under the tenant's own name.

03 Provider activates service

Electricity, gas, and internet are arranged directly with the provider.

04 Tenant keeps records

Save account details, confirmation emails, and reference numbers.

Important: Utility connection is the tenant's responsibility and is outside the leasing team's service scope. The agency can provide general guidance, but does not open utility accounts or manage tenant utility bills.
