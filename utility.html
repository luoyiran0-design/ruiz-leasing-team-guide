Before Move-In

Utility Connection Guide

Use this page after your tenancy agreement has been fully signed. Prepare your move-in date, property details, ID, and contact information before arranging electricity, gas, internet, or any building-specific service.

Return to Main Guide

Start Here

What to Prepare

01 Confirm your agreement is complete

Utility setup should be arranged after the agreement has been signed and your move-in date is confirmed.

02 Prepare your property details

Have your full property address, move-in date, full name, phone number, and email address ready.

03 Choose the services you need

Most residents arrange electricity and internet. Gas may only apply to some properties.

04 Save confirmation records

Keep confirmation emails, reference numbers, and connection dates in case you need to follow up.

Connection Types

Common Utilities

ElectricityUsually required before or on move-in day.
GasOnly required if the property uses gas appliances or gas hot water.
InternetArrange early if you need internet active close to move-in.
Embedded NetworkSome apartments have building-specific providers. Check your welcome information or ask the Property Manager after handover.
