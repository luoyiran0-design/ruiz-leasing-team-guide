Bond Payment

How to Pay Your Bond

To confirm your tenancy, your tenancy agreement must be signed and your bond payment must be completed as instructed in the email titled "Time to sign your tenancy agreement".

01Use the bond details in your signing email

Bond payment details are included in the same email as your tenancy agreement.

02Enter the correct payment reference

The reference number must be entered in the payment description so your bond can be allocated correctly.

03Keep proof of payment

Save your receipt, screenshot, or confirmation number until your tenancy has been confirmed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your tenancy agreement is sent in the email titled "Time to sign your tenancy agreement". Search the inbox linked to your rental application. If you do not see it, check spam, junk, promotions, and any secondary inboxes.

First check spam, junk, promotions, and any secondary inboxes. If you still cannot find it, contact the leasing team with your full name, unit number, phone number, and the email address used on your application so we can verify the file and resend the link if needed.

Open the signing link, review the tenancy agreement carefully, and complete every required initial, checkbox, and signature field. If there is more than one applicant or guarantor, each person may need to sign from their own email link before the agreement is fully complete.

Use the bond payment details included in the same email as your tenancy agreement. When making the payment, enter the correct payment reference number in the description field so the bond can be allocated correctly.

Your tenancy is confirmed when your tenancy agreement has been fully signed and your bond payment has been completed as instructed. Keep your payment receipt or confirmation number for your records.

Download the Sorted app on your mobile device. iPhone users can download it from the App Store, and Android users can download it from Google Play. Log in using the email address associated with your rental application, then go to Account > Wallet > Payment methods and add your bank card.
